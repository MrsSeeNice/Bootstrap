Objective
The objective is to safely walk your dog while enjoying it. First, call your dog to your front dog and tell him or her to sit. Next, take your dog's collar and place it aroung his or her neck with just enough space for two of your finger to fit between the neck and collar. Clip the leash (or lead) to the collar.
